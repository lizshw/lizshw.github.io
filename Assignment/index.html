Gehry Getty Interactive Experience Analysis

Liz Shaw - s4074048

Selected Project: Gehry Getty

URL: Gehry Getty

Interaction Experience

1. First Attention

The first thing that caught my attention was the dynamic 3D model of the Getty Center, which was the main feature of the experience.

2. Discrete Actions

  • Hovered over the 3D model to rotate and zoom in.
  • Clicked on specific sections of the Getty Center to learn more.
  • Switched between different architectural layers.
  • Examined the textures and materials in detail.

3. Most Engaging Part

I spent the most time exploring the 3D model, rotating and zooming in on the intricate architectural features.

4. Most Common Action

Rotating and zooming the 3D model to examine different angles.

Experience Goals

5. Primary Goal

The experience aims to engage users in exploring the architectural design of the Getty Center through an interactive, immersive 3D model.

6. Communication of Goal

The 3D model and interactive controls clearly communicate that the goal is to explore the architecture in depth.

Interaction Over Time

7. Expected Interaction Duration

The experience is designed for short, repeated interactions rather than a single long session.

8. Communication of Expected Interaction

Visual cues, highlighted sections, and interactive elements suggest users should explore at their own pace.

Media References

9. Referenced Media

The experience references digital architectural visualization and 3D modeling techniques used in architecture and design.

10. Communication of Actions

The reference suggests that users should interact with the model as an exploration tool, focusing on design details.

11. Communication of Emotions

Users should feel engaged and curious, discovering the thoughtfulness of the design.

Interaction Evaluation

12. Most Frustrating Part

Zooming in and out can be slow or imprecise, making it difficult to focus on small details.

13. Most Satisfying Part

Exploring the textures and materials up close is very rewarding and immersive.
